  Embark on a culinary journey of oceanic delights at Cafe "Light," where our seafood menu is a testament to freshness and the expertise of our seasoned chef, who honed her skills in Singapore for eight years. Here's a glimpse of our exquisite seafood offerings:
1. Calamari Koloke
Experience the perfect blend of crispy and tender with our Calamari Koloke, showcasing the succulence of fresh calamari in a flavorful batter.
2. Fried Noodles Seafood
Delight in the symphony of flavors and textures in our Fried Noodles Seafood, where an array of fresh seafood is expertly stir-fried to perfection.
3. Fried Rice Seafood
Elevate your fried rice experience with our Fried Rice Seafood, featuring a medley of the finest catches from the sea.
4. Fried Kwetiau Seafood
Indulge in the unique texture of kwetiau combined with the freshness of seafood in our Fried Kwetiau Seafood, a dish that promises a delightful crunch in every bite.
5. Noodles Soup Milk Seafood
Warm your soul with the comforting richness of our Noodles Soup Milk Seafood, a hearty and satisfying bowl that highlights the bounty of the sea.
6. Kwetiau Soup Seafood
Immerse yourself in the savory goodness of our Kwetiau Soup Seafood, where the delicate flavors of seafood harmonize with the fragrant broth.
7. Tuna Tofu Teriyaki
Savor the fusion of Japanese flavors in our Tuna Tofu Teriyaki, where succulent tuna meets the velvety goodness of teriyaki.
8. Sandwich Tuna Salad
Enjoy a light and refreshing option with our Sandwich Tuna Salad, a perfect blend of flaky tuna and crisp, vibrant greens.
9. Avocado Seafood Salad
Refresh your palate with our Avocado Seafood Salad, a medley of creamy avocado and fresh seafood tossed in a zesty dressing.
10. Tuna Teriyaki
Dive into the delightful combination of sweet and savory with our Tuna Teriyaki, a dish that brings out the best in fresh tuna.
11. Tuna Steak
Experience the hearty goodness of Tuna Steak, perfectly seared to lock in the natural flavors and juices.
12. Tom Yum Seafood
Spice up your taste buds with our Tom Yum Seafood, a Thai-inspired soup that combines the boldness of tom yum with the richness of seafood.
13. Capcay Seafood
Embark on a flavorful journey with our Capcay Seafood, a colorful stir-fry that highlights the freshness of assorted seafood and vegetables.
14. Tom Kha Seafood
Immerse yourself in the aromatic bliss of our Tom Kha Seafood, a coconut milk-based soup that brings out the delicate flavors of seafood.
15. Palmeni Fish
Enjoy a taste of Russian culinary influence with our Palmeni Fish, a dish that combines the comfort of dumplings with the richness of fish.
16. Fish Soup
