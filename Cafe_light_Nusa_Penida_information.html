  Welcome to Cafe "Light," a hidden gem located just 2 km before the breathtaking "Crystal Bay Beach" on the mesmerizing island of Nusa Penida. Nestled in front of the charming "Penida Lapaya Garden" hotel, our cafe offers a delightful dining experience with a serene ambiance. At Cafe "Light," take pride in our five cozy tables where you can enjoy a culinary journey curated by our talented chef, who brings eight years of expertise from Singapore to Nusa Penida. Our extensive menu caters to diverse palates, featuring delectable options ranging from cakes, seafood, and meat dishes to refreshing European and Chinese cuisine. Operating from 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM, we are committed to serving only the freshest ingredients, ensuring an unforgettable dining experience for our guests.
